Overview

EcoGlow Skincareis a Johannesburg-based South African SME that creates eco-friendly and natural skincare products made from locally obtained components. This blog covers the creation of a professional website to help EcoGlow's online expansion, increase consumer involvement, and provide safe e-commerce capabilities.

Goals of the Website

  • Create a powerful, identifiable online brand. Allow simple access to product and brand information. Increase customer trust and loyalty. Increase engagement with blogs, newsletters, and reviews.

Our EcpGlow Skincare Features

  • Homepage our featured products
  • Interactive product catalog
  • Contact page
  • Mobile-friendly (responsive) design

Design and User Experience

The website will use natural tones (greens, whites, earthy textures) to reflect the brand's eco-friendly values. Navigation will be simple and intuitive, using fonts like Lato or Open Sans. The site will load quickly and provide a smooth checkout process across all devices.
